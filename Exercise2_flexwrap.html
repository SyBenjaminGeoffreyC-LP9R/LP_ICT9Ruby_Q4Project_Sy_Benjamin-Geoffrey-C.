Rick Riordan's Books

Percy Jackson and the Olympians (PJO)

The Percy Jackson and the Olympian series (PJO) is a story about a young boy named Percy who discovers that he is the son of a Greek god. And Percy is not just the son of a random Greek god, no, He is the son of Poseidon, one of the Big 3. This series follows along the "Great Prophecy" in which it states that a child of the Big 3 (Zeus, Poseidon, and Hades) has the choice whether to save or destroy Olympus. Will Percy be the one from the prophecy, and will his desicions cause Olympus' preservation or destruction? Read along as Percy travels through the mythological world, having adventures an quests, and making some friends and enemies along the way.

Heroes of Olympus (HOO)

This story is the sequel to the Percy Jackson and the Olympians Series. This series follows along the "Prophecy of 7" wherein 7 demigods have to work together in order to save the world from Gaea (also known as the Primordial of the Earth) and the Giants. This series revolves around 7 demigods who travel together throughout the world to prevent Gaea from being woken up, as well as stopping the Giants from destroying Olympus. Read along as the 7 demigods encounter foes and challenges as they try to prevent Gaea's awakening.

Trials of Apollo (TOA)

This series occurs after the events of the Heroes of Olympus series (HOO). This follows Apollo, who has been stripped of his godhood by Zeus, after he took the blame for the war against Gaea and the Giants. Read along as Apollo finishes trials with his sworn demigod partner, technically master, Meg. Will Apollo be able to finish all his trials and regain his godhood while learning things about being a human along the way?
